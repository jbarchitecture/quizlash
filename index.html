The Parched Killer

A criminal kidnaps people and demands them to take one of the two pills that he offers. The pills look identical, but the killer claims that only one is poisonous. When a victim chooses a pill, the killer takes the remaining one. The victims always die after swallowing the tablet with water.

Volatile Chemicals

A renowned chemist is found dead in his lab. There is no clear evidence except a piece of paper lying by the body. The paper is blank other than the name of five elements scrawled across it hastily:

Nickel, Carbon, Oxygen, Lanthanum, Sulfur

Three people visited the chemist that day – his sister, Lanaya, his colleague, Nicolas, and his wife, Teresa.
